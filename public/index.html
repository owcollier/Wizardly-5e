Wizardly 5e ~

A simple D&D 5th edition app to help you manage your wizard's spell book.

Wizard Stats

Your wizard's relevant casting stats, stored right alongside your spell book.

Your Spell Book

All your known spells in one place, organized by casting level. Manage your beloved spell book without the pen and paper.

Spell Reference

Convenient access to details on 200+ fifth edition Wizard spells. Check out spell descriptions directly from your spell book.

Track Prepared Spells

Easy prepared spell tracking. Toggle prepared status with one touch. It's like magic!
